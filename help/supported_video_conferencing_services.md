At the moment **In Your Face** supports the following services:

* Google Hangouts
* Google Meet
* Zoom
* WebEx
* Microsoft Teams
* goTo Meeting
* BlueJeans
* join.me
* Whereby
* RingCentral
* Amazon Chime
* Apple FaceTime
* Jitsi Meet
* YouCanBookMe
* Entre
* Riverside
* Slack Huddles
* Meta Workplace
* Skype

If you miss a conferencing service please drop a message at [iyf@bluebanana-software.com](mailto:iyf@bluebanana-software.com)
